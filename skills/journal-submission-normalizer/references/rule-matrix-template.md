## Status Meanings

- `pass`: compliant before editing or verified after editing.
- `fixed`: changed during normalization.
- `needs author`: missing factual content that only the author can provide.
- `unknown`: official rule not found or contradictory.
- `not applicable`: rule does not apply to this article type.
