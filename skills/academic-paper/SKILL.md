# Academic Paper Router

Use this alias for academic paper writing tasks.

## Route

1. Use `paper-spine` when the manuscript needs research question, contribution, evidence chain, section function, or draft architecture.
2. Use `nature-writing` for manuscript sections, abstracts, cover letters, and journal-style scientific prose.
3. Use `nature-polishing` for language refinement after the argument and evidence are stable.
4. Use `nature-citation` and `reference-checker` for citation integrity and reference formatting.
5. Use `research-integrity-guardrail` or `paper-self-review` before submission-quality claims.
