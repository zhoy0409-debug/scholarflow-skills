# Flash And Pro Research

Use this reference to decide research depth.

## Flash

Collect:

- 3 target-scene examples.
- 3 recent high-quality field examples.
- Official requirements from the target authority.

Use flash when the user wants speed, early diagnosis, a first rewrite, or does
not yet have enough materials for deep style learning.

## Pro

Collect:

- 6 target-scene examples.
- 6 recent high-quality field examples.
- Official requirements from the target authority.

Use pro when the user asks for publishable or competition-level quality, final
submission support, or deep imitation.

## Selection Rules

Target-scene examples show how the target evaluates and formats work. Field
examples show how the topic area argues, reports evidence, and states claims.
Do not mix their roles.

For every selected example, record:

- source,
- year,
- relevance,
- reason selected,
- section-level writing feature to learn,
- constraints for transfer.
