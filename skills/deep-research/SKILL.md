# Deep Research Router

Use this skill as a lightweight alias for rigorous literature investigation and evidence mapping.

## Route

1. Use `nature-academic-search` to define search terms, databases, filters, and inclusion criteria.
2. Use `nature-literature-pipeline` and `zotero-lit-fetch` to collect, organize, and preserve source metadata.
3. Use `nature-reader` for close reading and evidence extraction.
4. Use `reference-checker` and `nature-citation` for citation verification.
5. Use `paper-spine-research` or `paper-spine` when the output must become a manuscript argument.
