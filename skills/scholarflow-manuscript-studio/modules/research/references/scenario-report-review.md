# Scenario: Report Or Review

Research reports and reviews as evaluation-driven documents. This scenario
covers course reports, technical reports, research training reports, survey
papers, and review-style deliverables.

## Official Sources

Find and record when available:

- school, department, course, or project page,
- assignment instructions,
- rubric,
- required section order,
- formatting requirements,
- citation requirements,
- review scope and expected depth,
- grading or evaluation criteria.

## Example Learning

Learn:

- expected audience knowledge,
- balance between background and contribution,
- how evidence is organized,
- how methods or literature are synthesized,
- what makes the report persuasive under the rubric.

Do not force reports into a journal-paper structure unless the target requires
it.
