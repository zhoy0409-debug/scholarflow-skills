# Manuscript Writing Router

Use this alias for manuscript drafting and revision.

## Route

1. For Nature-family manuscript style, load `nature-writing` first.
2. For general academic paper workflows, load `paper-spine` to stabilize the argument, section function, evidence chain, and revision plan.
3. For polishing, load `nature-polishing`.
4. For references, load `nature-citation`.
5. For responses to reviewers, load `nature-response`.
