# Deep Research Review Router

Use this alias when the user asks for `deep-research review` or asks to review a literature review, evidence map, or research synthesis.

## Route

1. Use `reference-checker` and `nature-citation` for source identity, citation integrity, and claim-source alignment.
2. Use `nature-reviewer` when the output is manuscript-like or needs reviewer simulation.
3. Use `paper-self-review` for structure, contribution, evidence chain, and revision priority.
4. Use `research-integrity-guardrail` when the review involves unsupported claims, disclosure, ethics, or overstatement risk.
5. Use `nature-reader` to re-read any source that is central to the argument.
